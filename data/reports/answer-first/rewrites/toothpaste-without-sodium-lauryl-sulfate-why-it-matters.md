Sodium lauryl sulfate, or SLS, is a harsh foaming agent found in most conventional toothpastes that can irritate your gums, cause canker sores, and leave a weird taste in your mouth. A toothpaste without SLS cleans just as well using gentler ingredients instead. Ours uses coconut oil, baking soda, peppermint and spearmint essential oils, and myrrh powder to freshen your breath and keep your teeth clean without the irritation.
