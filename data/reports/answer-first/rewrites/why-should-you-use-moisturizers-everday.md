Your skin needs moisture every single day, even if you think it's oily. A good moisturizer does three things: it adds water back into your skin, it locks that water in so it doesn't evaporate, and it helps your skin stay strong enough to protect itself from irritants and bacteria. Skip it, and your skin gets tight, flaky, and way more prone to breakouts and irritation. Whether you're dealing with eczema, psoriasis, or just regular dryness, a daily moisturizer is the difference between skin that feels comfortable and skin that's constantly angry.
