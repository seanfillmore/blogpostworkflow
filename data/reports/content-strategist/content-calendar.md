# Content Calendar — Real Skin Care

## Strategic Rationale

This 8-week calendar is built around three principles drawn directly from the gap analysis: **lead with zero-competition volume**, **build cluster authority before branching out**, and **introduce TOF content systematically to widen the top of RSC's funnel**.

Weeks 1–2 are dedicated entirely to the fluoride-free toothpaste cluster, which represents the single most disproportionate opportunity in RSC's entire content library — 22,000+ monthly searches at KD 0 where RSC sits at position 46. These posts require minimal competitive research and have the highest probability of ranking within 30–60 days. The toothpaste cluster then continues through Weeks 3–4 as supporting SLS/sodium lauryl sulfate content is layered in, each piece reinforcing the others through internal links.

Weeks 3–4 also introduce the lip balm cluster from scratch — a completely unaddressed product category at 4,900/mo KD 6. Lip balm and toothpaste content can cross-link through shared "clean beauty" and "natural personal care" framing, building broader topical authority.

Weeks 5–6 open the bar soap category with gender-targeted roundups and a parent-pillar guide, then fold in the deodorant gap piece (kids deodorant, KD 0) which completes RSC's existing deodorant cluster. Body lotion content, already partially covered by RSC, is slotted into Weeks 7–8 where consolidated "natural lotion" and "organic body lotion" posts round out the consideration-stage inventory.

TOF lifestyle editorial begins in Week 4 and runs at a rate of one piece per two-week block — a sustainable cadence for a small team that ensures the top of the funnel is being opened without crowding out the high-priority MOF quick wins. By Week 8, RSC will have published 20 posts covering all six major content categories, established pillar-and-spoke cluster structures in toothpaste, lip balm, and bar soap from nothing, and created its first systematic TOF editorial content.
