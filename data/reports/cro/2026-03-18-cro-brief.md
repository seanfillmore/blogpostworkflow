# CRO Brief — 2026-03-18
**Generated:** March 18, 2026

## Summary
Yesterday (March 18) the store received only **7 real sessions** out of 40 total (82.5% bot traffic), generated **zero orders and zero abandoned checkouts**, and averaged just **1.12 pages per session** — meaning almost every real visitor bounced after one page. A 15% script-error rate suggests broken functionality that could be silently killing conversions. The immediate priorities are fixing the technical issues, filtering/blocking bot traffic to get clean data, and improving the on-page experience to convert the small trickle of organic visitors.

## Action Items

### 1. Fix JavaScript Errors Immediately — HIGH
**Evidence:** Script error rate = 15% of sessions; with only 7 real sessions, even one broken experience is catastrophic.
**Action:** Open Clarity → Errors tab and identify the specific JS errors. Check your theme's cart/add-to-cart, product image gallery, and any third-party apps (reviews, pop-ups). Test on both Chrome mobile and desktop. Fix or remove the offending script today — a broken add-to-cart or checkout flow means zero chance of conversion.

### 2. Block / Filter Bot Traffic — HIGH
**Evidence:** 33 of 40 sessions (82.5%) are bots; 9 sessions from China suggest crawler/scraper activity. "Distinct users" showing 72 vs. 40 sessions confirms inflated, unreliable analytics.
**Action:** Install Cloudflare (free tier) or enable Shopify's bot-protection features. Add a rule to challenge traffic from known bot user-agents and suspicious geos (China accounted for 22.5% of sessions with near-zero purchase intent). This will give you clean data to make real CRO decisions.

### 3. Improve Blog-to-Product Conversion Path — HIGH
**Evidence:** Blog/content pages ("Best Toothpaste Without SLS," "Tea Tree Oil Benefits," "Coconut Oil Body Lotion Roundup") drove at least 6 of 40 sessions, yet pages per session = 1.12 — visitors read the article and leave.
**Action:** Add a prominent, above-the-fold product CTA card (with image, price, and "Shop Now" button) inside each blog post, linking to the relevant product page. For the SLS-free toothpaste article → link to the SLS Free Toothpaste collection. For the coconut lotion roundup → link to the Coconut Lotion PDP. Use anchor-style inline CTAs every 300-400 words as well.

### 4. Increase Scroll Depth on Key Pages — MED
**Evidence:** Average scroll depth = 45.23%. Visitors are seeing less than half the page content, missing product benefits, reviews, and add-to-cart sections on longer pages.
**Action:** On the homepage and top product pages (Coconut Lotion, Coconut Soap), move the strongest value proposition, social proof, and primary CTA above the fold. Use sticky add-to-cart bar on mobile (14 of 40 sessions are mobile). Shorten hero sections and remove decorative whitespace that pushes content down.

### 5. Optimize the Coconut Lotion PDP — MED
**Evidence:** Coconut Lotion product page had the 2nd-highest session count (3 sessions) among real landing pages but produced 0 add-to-carts and 0 orders.
**Action:** Audit the PDP: ensure the price is visible without scrolling, add urgency copy ("Only X left" or free-shipping threshold), include 2-3 short customer testimonials near the buy button, and add a benefit-driven bullet list (e.g., "Only 6 ingredients · No parabens · 48-hour moisture"). If you don't have reviews, add a founder's note or a "Why customers love this" blurb.

### 6. Build an Email Capture for Blog Visitors — MED
**Evidence:** 0 orders, 0 abandoned carts, but content pages are driving the majority of real traffic — these visitors are in research mode and won't buy on the first visit.
**Action:** Add a non-intrusive slide-in or exit-intent popup offering a 10% first-order discount in exchange for an email. Target it to blog pages only. Use Klaviyo or Shopify Email to set up a 3-email welcome sequence: (1) deliver discount, (2) educate on ingredient philosophy, (3) product recommendation. This turns today's zero-conversion blog traffic into future purchasers.

### 7. Invest in Driving More Qualified Traffic — LOW
**Evidence:** Only 7 real sessions in a full day — no CRO tactic can generate meaningful revenue at this volume.
**Action:** Prioritize one traffic channel: double down on SEO for the blog content that's already ranking (SLS-free toothpaste, tea tree oil, coconut lotion), or launch a small Meta/Google Ads campaign ($10-20/day) targeting "natural toothpaste" and "coconut body lotion" keywords. Aim for at least 50 real sessions/day before investing heavily in on-site optimization.
