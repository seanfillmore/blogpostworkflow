**Action:** Configure Shopify's automated abandoned checkout email sequence (3 emails: 1hr, 24hr, 72hr) with a small discount in the final email. Also enable Shopify's checkout recovery SMS if you collect phone numbers. Get this live now so it's ready when traffic arrives.
