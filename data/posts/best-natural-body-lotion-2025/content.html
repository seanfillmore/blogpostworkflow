What Makes a Lotion Truly “Natural”?

While “natural” isn’t a regulated term in the U.S., the best natural lotions share key qualities:

  • Plant-based oils & butters: Coconut oil, jojoba oil, shea butter, and red palm oil nourish without clogging pores.
  • Free from harsh chemicals: No parabens, phthalates, sulfates, or drying alcohols.
  • Minimal processing: Ingredients retain more nutrients when not heavily refined.
  • Gentle preservation: Natural options like grapefruit seed extract keep formulas safe without synthetic preservatives.
  • Sustainable & cruelty-free: Ethical sourcing benefits both your skin and the planet.

Why Avoid Harsh Ingredients?

Many mainstream lotions contain ingredients that can strip moisture or trigger irritation:

  • Drying alcohols: Can damage the skin barrier and cause tightness over time.
  • Petroleum derivatives: Create an occlusive layer that doesn’t let skin “breathe.”
  • Synthetic fragrance: A common trigger for allergic reactions and eczema flare-ups.
