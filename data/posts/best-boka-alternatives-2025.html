Boka is one of the most talked-about natural toothpaste brands — known for its sleek packaging and dentist-backed hydroxyapatite formula. But many moms are looking for alternatives to Boka — options that are more affordable, simpler in ingredients, or more family-friendly. The good news? In 2025, there are plenty of excellent SLS-Free, non-toxic toothpaste options to choose from. This guide highlights the best Boka alternatives for your family!

Quick Look at the Best Boka Alternatives

1: Real Skin Care Toothpaste

Real Skin Care Toothpaste is a fantastic Boka alternative. It uses an organic coconut oil base and baking soda for natural whitening, with absolutely no SLS, synthetic sweeteners, or artificial flavors. Handmade in the USA, it’s designed for daily use for both kids and adults — and is much more affordable than premium options like Boka.

2: David’s Natural Toothpaste

David’s Natural Toothpaste is a premium but more affordable option compared to Boka. It’s EWG Verified, fluoride-free, and packaged in sustainable aluminum. Many families love David’s for its clean ingredients and excellent natural whitening power.

3: Risewell Mineral Toothpaste

Risewell Mineral Toothpaste is another popular alternative to Boka, featuring hydroxyapatite to help strengthen enamel — the same star ingredient Boka uses. It’s SLS-Free, dentist-formulated, and growing fast in the clean oral care space.

4: Hello Naturally Whitening Toothpaste (SLS-Free)

Hello SLS-Free Toothpaste is a budget-friendly choice that’s widely available at Target, Walmart, and Amazon. It’s a great option for families who want SLS-Free toothpaste with fun flavors, though it isn’t quite as premium in ingredients as Boka or David’s.

5: Dr. Bronner’s All-One Toothpaste

Dr. Bronner’s All-One Toothpaste is a great choice for organic shoppers. USDA Organic certified, SLS-Free, and made with a coconut oil base, it’s a very gentle and mild alternative to Boka — ideal for those wanting the simplest possible formula.

6: Tom’s of Maine SLS-Free Toothpaste

Tom’s of Maine SLS-Free Toothpaste is an affordable and trusted brand. It’s not as premium or sleek as Boka, but it’s readily available and an easy way for families to switch to SLS-Free toothpaste without breaking the bank.

7: Redmond Earthpaste Natural Toothpaste

Redmond Earthpaste is one of the cleanest formulas on the market. It’s SLS-Free, fluoride-free, glycerin-free, and features bentonite clay as its base. It’s a minimalist option for those who want the most stripped-down natural toothpaste available.
