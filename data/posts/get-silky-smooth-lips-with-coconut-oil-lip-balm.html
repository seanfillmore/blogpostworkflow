What Makes Coconut Oil Lip Balm Different?

Many conventional balms rely on petrolatum and artificial flavors that can feel slick at first, but don’t actually help your lips hold moisture. A truly natural lip balm focuses on skin-supportive oils and waxes that mimic the skin’s own barrier. Our approach is simple: fewer ingredients, higher quality, made in small batches for maximum freshness and consistency.

Ingredients & Why They Matter

  • Organic Virgin Coconut Oil – A rich source of medium-chain fatty acids that melt on contact, soften rough texture, and support a healthy moisture barrier. Coconut oil’s natural emolliency makes it ideal for dry, flaky lips and cold-weather care.
  • Organic Beeswax – Creates a breathable, protective seal to lock in hydration and shield against wind and cold without a heavy or sticky feel. It’s the classic partner to oils for long-lasting comfort.
  • Organic Red Palm Oil – Naturally rich in carotenoids and vitamin E, this antioxidant-dense oil supports supple, resilient lips and a smoother look over time.

Together, these three ingredients deliver a balanced blend of slip, nourishment, and occlusion—so your lips feel moisturized now and stay comfortable for hours.

How to Use & Reapply

  1. AM/PM basics: Apply a thin, even layer in the morning and before bed. At night, add a slightly thicker coat for an overnight moisture mask.
  2. Before makeup: Apply a light layer, wait 2–3 minutes, then blot once before lipstick for a smoother finish.
  3. Cold weather & travel: Reapply every few hours when air is dry (winter, flights, beach days).
  4. Post-sun care: After sun exposure, cleanse lips with water, pat dry, then apply balm to comfort and hydrate.
