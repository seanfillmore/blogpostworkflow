How to Use Coconut Body Cream for Best Results

Apply a pea- to almond-sized amount to clean, damp skin to lock in water and amplify hydration. Massage until absorbed. Try:

  • Body: elbows, knees, heels, hands — anywhere that feels tight or rough.
  • Face: as a targeted comfort layer for dry patches (always patch test first).
  • Over lotion: layer on top of a lightweight moisturizer for extra sealing power.
  • Post-shave: calm and re-hydrate freshly shaved areas.

Build a Simple, Effective Moisture Routine

  1. Cleanse gently. Avoid harsh surfactants that strip your barrier.
  2. Apply on damp skin. Within 2–3 minutes of bathing, when skin still feels dewy.
  3. Layer smartly. Use Coconut Body Lotion for slip and light hydration, then seal with cream where you need more.
  4. Be consistent. Daily use keeps the barrier resilient and comfortable.

Unscented First (Then Explore Scents)

We recommend starting with unscented if you have sensitive skin. If you try scented options later, always patch test new aromas behind the ear or on the inner arm for 24–48 hours before wider use.

Storage & Care

  • Store at room temperature away from direct heat or sun.
  • Use clean, dry hands or a spatula to avoid introducing water into the jar.
  • As with any new product, patch test if your skin is very reactive.
