Your lips are one of the most sensitive areas of your skin and are constantly exposed to sun, wind, and dry air. Unlike most skin, lips do not contain oil glands, which means they rely entirely on external moisture and protection. Without proper care, they can quickly become dry, chapped, and prone to cracking. Using an organic lip balm every day helps prevent dryness, repair damage, and protect against long-term issues — keeping your lips soft, hydrated, and healthy year-round.
