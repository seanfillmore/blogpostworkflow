Frequently Asked Questions

Why switch from Dr. Bronner’s to an alternative?
Many of Dr. Bronner’s lotions are heavily scented with essential oils, which some families find too strong—especially for babies, pregnancy, or scent-free workplaces. The alternatives here emphasize gentler or unscented formulas.

Is “unscented” always fragrance-free?
Not necessarily. Some brands use masking agents to neutralize odor. If you’re sensitive, choose products labeled fragrance-free and scan the ingredient list for perfumes or essential oils.

What makes Real Skin Care different?
A short, transparent ingredient list—purified spring water, organic coconut oil, jojoba, red palm oil, and beeswax—without fragrance, masking agents, parabens, or alcohols. It’s handmade and designed for sensitive, family use.

Can I use these lotions on kids or during pregnancy?
Fragrance-free, minimal-ingredient formulas are often preferred for kids and during pregnancy. Always patch test and consult a healthcare provider for specific concerns.

How do I patch test a new lotion?
Apply a small amount to the inner forearm once daily for 2–3 days. If no redness, itching, or irritation appears, start regular use.
