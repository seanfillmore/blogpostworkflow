What Is Coconut Oil Bar Soap?

Coconut oil bar soap is a traditional, cold-processed soap made by saponifying high-quality oils. At Real Skin Care, our Unscented Bar Soap is made from saponified organic virgin coconut oil—that’s it. No dyes, no artificial fragrance, and no mystery preservatives.

How to Use Coconut Oil Bar Soap

  1. Wet and lather: Rub the bar between hands or on a washcloth to build creamy foam.
  2. Cleanse gently: Massage onto skin in circular motions; rinse with lukewarm water.
  3. Pat dry & moisturize: Follow with Coconut Body Lotion or Organic Body Cream to lock in hydration.

Who It’s Best For

  • Sensitive or reactive skin: Minimal formula avoids common irritants.
  • Dry or normal skin: Gentle cleanse that won’t leave skin tight.
  • Family use: A straightforward, clean bar for everyday bathing.

Note: If you’re acne-prone, patch test before frequent facial use and follow with a non-comedogenic moisturizer.

Care, Storage & Longevity

  • Use a draining soap dish to keep the bar dry between uses.
  • Keep out of standing water; store spare bars in a cool, dry place.
  • For travel, cut the bar in half to extend life and reduce waste.
